    \textbf{Real-world events leave visible traces:} after the 2024 \emph{polyfill.io} supply-chain compromise, affected government sites quickly dropped it. After Russia's 2022 invasion of Ukraine, Russian sites with third-party trackers fell from \textbf{62.9\% to 44\%}, with \emph{google-analytics.com} disappearing entirely from RU sites post-2022. Privacy-centric analytics (Matomo, Plausible, Fathom) appear only \textbf{from 2021 onwards}, mainly in GDPR/Schrems~II jurisdictions.
